About FitCore Gym

Empowering fitness journeys since 2018

Our Story

FitCore Gym was founded with a simple mission: to create an inclusive, supportive environment where everyone can achieve their fitness goals. What started as a small community gym has grown into Nairobi's premier fitness destination.

Our journey began when our founder, Sarah Kimani, noticed the lack of accessible, professional fitness facilities in the area. Today, we serve over 2,000 members and continue to expand our services.

Our Mission

We believe fitness should be accessible, enjoyable, and sustainable. Our expert team is dedicated to helping you build healthy habits that last a lifetime.

Fitness Terminology

Reps
The number of times you perform a specific exercise movement
Set
A group of consecutive repetitions of an exercise
BMI
A measure of body fat based on height and weight
Progressive Overload
Gradually increasing the weight, frequency, or number of repetitions in your strength training routine
HIIT
A training technique alternating short periods of intense exercise with recovery periods
